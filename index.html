§01 / Mission

Our Mission.

We're here to push the industry forward by lifting each other up: swapping real stories, bold experiments, honest lessons, and practical strategies that move the needle for everyone.

Through monthly talks, tactical deep dives, idea jams, and open peer support, we've built a space where game marketing and publishing pros can trade what's actually working (and what's not), across launches, growth campaigns, and GTM strategies.

FirstLook.gg is a player relationship platform built for modern game studios. It unifies player growth, insights, and operations, helping studios build stronger, longer lasting relationships with their communities.

IndieBI

IndieBI is a comprehensive business intelligence platform for game developers and publishers. It provides real time sales tracking, advanced analytics, and actionable insights to maximize commercial success.
